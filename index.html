About SafeHaven

Our mission is to provide support and safety when you need it most

The Story Behind SafeHaven

I'm a single father who started with nothing but a dream to make a difference. Growing up with limited resources taught me the value of resilience and community support.

The idea for SafeHaven came from my own experiences navigating difficult times. I realized how valuable it would have been to have a single resource that connected people with all the support services available to them.

SafeHaven began as a personal project to help people find resources during challenging times. As a self-taught developer, each feature represents countless hours of learning and determination.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about SafeHaven

Is SafeHaven free to download?

Yes, SafeHaven is free to download and includes access to the resource finder. Premium features like the emergency SOS and journal require a subscription.

How does the Emergency feature work?

The Emergency feature uses a specially designed slider that requires deliberate action to prevent accidental calls. When activated, it calls emergency services and sends your location to pre-designated contacts.

Is my journal information private?

Absolutely. Your journal entries are stored only on your device and are not uploaded to our servers. Your privacy is our priority.

How are resources verified?

We work to ensure all listed resources are active and available. Resources are regularly reviewed and updated based on community feedback and direct verification.

Can I cancel my premium subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID settings. Your premium features will remain active until the end of your billing cycle.

How can I suggest a resource?

We welcome community input! Use the contact form below to suggest new resources, and our team will review them for inclusion in the app.
